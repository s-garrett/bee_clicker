Jeremy's Will

  • click on the bees before they reach you in the center of the map
  • each bee that hits you will reduce your health.
  • clicking on a mushroom will give you more health.
  • the game is over when you run out of health or time.
  • by clicking on the stumps that appear you can gain more time.
